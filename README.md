# Sri Selva Vinayakar Koyil — Brisbane

The official website of **Sri Selva Vinayakar Koyil**, a traditional Hindu Ganesha temple
located in South Maclean, Queensland — about 35 km south of Brisbane, beside the Logan River.

🌐 **Live site:** https://ssvk.naaglabs.workers.dev

## About the Temple

Consecrated on 5 February 1995, Sri Selva Vinayakar Koyil has served the Tamil Hindu
community of South-East Queensland for three decades. The temple is built in the
traditional South Indian style in accordance with ancient Sitpa Sastra and Agamas.
